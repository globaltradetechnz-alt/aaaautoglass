Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Answers to the questions we hear most before booking.

We offer same-day service across Greater Auckland in most cases. Call us on 0800 757-985 and we'll get a technician to you as fast as possible — often within hours.

Yes — we're fully mobile. Our technicians come to your home, workplace, or any location across Greater Auckland.

We work with 36+ NZ insurance providers. We direct bill your insurance company. If nil excess there is nothing for you to pay. If you have a windscreen excess, that is all you have to pay. Call us and we'll help you through the process.

We specialise in ADAS-equipped vehicles. We ensure all camera housings and sensors are correctly positioned after fitting and advise on calibration requirements.

Yes — they're our specialties. We carry OEM-quality glass for Tesla Model 3 and Y, and all Range Rover models including full HUD and sensor compatibility.

We use OEM-equivalent glass meeting or exceeding original factory specifications. For ADAS vehicles, we only fit windscreens compatible with your camera and sensor systems.
