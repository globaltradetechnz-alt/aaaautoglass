Our History

37 Years in the Making

AAA Auto Glass was established in 1989 with a simple mission: provide Auckland drivers with fast, reliable, expert auto glass service at a fair price. Over three and a half decades, that mission hasn't changed — even as the vehicles and technology around us have transformed.

What started as a straightforward windscreen replacement business has evolved into a specialist workshop capable of handling the most technologically advanced vehicles on NZ roads — from Tesla's roof glass panels to Range Rover HUD windscreens with ADAS camera systems.

We're based at 133 Pilkington Road, Panmure with a fully equipped workshop and one of New Zealand's largest auto glass inventories, holding over 40,000 pieces for rapid same-day service.

We work directly with 36+ insurance companies across New Zealand, handling claims end-to-end so you pay as little as possible out of pocket.
